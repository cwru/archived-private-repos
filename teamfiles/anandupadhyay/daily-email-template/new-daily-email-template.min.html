Tuition Exchange to continue at CWRU permanently, but changes coming to process

After completing a four-year pilot with the Tuition Exchange, a program that provides tuition assistance to dependents of university employees enrolled elsewhere, campus leaders have elected to continue the partnership on a permanent basis. The program proved popular among CWRU staff and faculty, and, based on preliminary data, the university is adjusting some of the processes involved in applying for and receiving aid.
