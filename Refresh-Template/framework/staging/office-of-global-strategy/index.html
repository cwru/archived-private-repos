About Our Office

The Office of Global Strategy works to further the international objectives and vision of the university by:

  • Developing strategies and direction for CWRU’s global engagement initiatives
  • Facilitating and building relationships with global partners
  • Documenting and communicating international activity externally and across campus

We also work to forge and maintain close connections with our international alumni and gaining insight and recommendations through our visiting committee.

Want to support our efforts? Find out how you can get involved.
