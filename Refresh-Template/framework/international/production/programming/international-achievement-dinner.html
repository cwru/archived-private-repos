International Achievement Dinner

An Evening Honoring Achievements

Case Western Reserve University honors members of the CWRU community and celebrates the numerous international achievements of our faculty members, students and staff members throughout the year. Organized by the Center for International Affairs, the International Achievement Dinner recognizes and honors:

Main carousel/image for page

Previous Next
  • Faculty Fulbright recipients
  • Fulbright Visiting Scholars
  • Faculty Seed Grant awardees
  • International Affairs Advisory Council members
  • Education Abroad Advisory Council members
  • A variety of representatives from CWRU multicultural organizations

Since 2014, prominent keynote speakers such as Dr. Allan E. Goodman, president and CEO of the Institute of International Education, and Ambassador Richard Armitage, former deputy secretary of state, have spoken to the community on internationalization in higher education.

It is thanks in part to the sponsorship and community support that CWRU is able to be a leader in international education.

The Center for International Affairs would like to thank previous sponsors: Cleveland Council on World Affairs, Global Cleveland, Margaret Wong & Associates LLC and The City Club of Cleveland.

The International Achievement Dinner is by invitation only. If you have questions, please contact internationalevents@case.edu.
