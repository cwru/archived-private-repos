Planning International Events

The Center for International Affairs offers support for students, faculty and staff with trainings, events and cultural celebrations. All events are based on strategic goals, such as campus internationalization, global citizenship, international student transitions, as well as faculty and staff professional development.

Our events include:

  • Asian Mid-Autumn Festival
  • Cleveland Bucket List
  • Global Talk Series for Faculty/Staff
  • Holi
  • International Achievement Dinner
  • International Education Week
  • International Graduation
  • International Student Success Series
  • Lunar New Year
  • Study Abroad Fair

Questions?

For more information about internationally focused events through the Center for International Affairs, please contact internationalevents@case.edu.
