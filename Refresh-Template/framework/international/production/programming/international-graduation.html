International Graduation Celebration

The Center for International Affairs celebrates graduating international students at a reception, just days before the graduation ceremony. It is an opportunity to celebrate their many accomplishments, reflect on their time at CWRU and hear remarks from President Barbara R. Snyder, Provost William "Bud" Baeslack III and Vice Provost for International Affairs David Fleshler. Graduating students also receive a certificate of congratulations from the office of City of Cleveland Mayor Frank G. Jackson.

The center is proud of all international graduates and excited to hear their plans ahead. The international student graduates represent dozens of countries, all degree levels and all seven schools and the college.

An RSVP is required. Invitations will be sent to graduating students via email. Questions? Email us at internationalevents@case.edu.
