International Student Success Series

Main carousel/image for page

Previous Next

The International Student Success Series (ISSS) is an extension of orientation programming and is designed to help international students transition and better understand the social and academic culture at CWRU. Each topic is designed to incorporate important life skills taught by knowledgeable trainers from across campus or from the community.

ISSS sessions are held every Thursday from 12-1pm in Tomlinson 135. All events are free and include food. Some will require registration.

Download the 2017-18 International Student Success Series schedule.

We are continually in the planning process for the next International Student Success Series. If you have ideas for future topic or if you would like to collaborate with a topic, please feel free to contact us at: internationalevents@case.edu.
