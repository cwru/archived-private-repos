Cecil Jowell

Director, Trencor Limited (Cape Town, South Africa)

Cecil Jowell is the executive director of Trencor Limited, which is listed on Johannesburg Stock Exchange. Additionally, he is director of Textainer Group Holdings Limited, which is listed on the New York Stock Exchange. He resides in Cape Town, South Africa.

Jowell received his undergraduate degree from the University of Cape Town and a graduate degree from the University of Cape Town. He continued his education at Harvard Business School in the Programme for Management Development.

Jowell was previously employed as chairman of Mobile Industries Ltd., an investment holding company and finance company. He held various positions at Henred Fruehauf Trailers Limited, Jowells Transport and Namaqualand Lugdiens, Namaqualand Garage, and he served as president of Motor Industries Federation of South Africa.
