James Ratner

Chairman of the Board, Forest City Realty Trust

Jim Ratner is Chairman of the Board and Director of Forest City Realty Trust.

Jim holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from Columbia University and a Master of Business Administration degree from Harvard University, Graduate School of Business Administration.

He is Chairman of the Board of the Playhouse Square Foundation, a member of the Board of Directors for NACCO Industries, Inc., Case Western Reserve University, the Cleveland Museum of Art, and Urban Land Institute.
