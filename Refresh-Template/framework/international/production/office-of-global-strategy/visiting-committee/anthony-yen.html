Anthony Yen

Chairman, Tenax Corp. (Cleveland, Ohio)

Anthony Yen has been a member of the Ohio Commodores Association, the Northern Ohio District Export Council Executive Committee, the Cleveland World Trade Association Board of Directors, the Greater Cleveland Growth Association Board of Directors, the Institute for Global Business Board of Directors at the University of Akron, and the Advisory Board at the Cleveland Institute of Art.

The consultancies in which Yen is involved include Preventive Oncology International Inc., the Visiting Committee of the College of Education and Human Services at Cleveland State University, and the Advisory Board of the Global Citizenship Center at Hathaway Brown School.

Yen has received many awards, including the Brilliant Star Award First Class By Republic of China on Taiwan in 1996, the World Trade Centers Association Tribute Recognition Award in 2001, and the U.S. Department of Commerce Award for 30 Years of Service in 2000.

He is an inductee of the Cleveland International Hall of Fame and is on the advisory board of the Greater Cleveland Chinese Chamber of Commerce.
