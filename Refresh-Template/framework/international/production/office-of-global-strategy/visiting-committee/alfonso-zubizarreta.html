Alfonso D. Zubizarreta

Vice President of International Strategy, Hyland Software (Westlake, Ohio)

As Vice President of International Strategy, Alfonso Zubizarreta is responsible for developing and identifying opportunities for Hyland, creator of OnBase's international growth and globalization efforts. Alfonso's 19 years of experience at Hyland, coupled with multiple executive roles, have given him deep knowledge of the ECM industry and Hyland’s OnBase solution.

Alfonso joined Hyland in 1993 as a senior developer, participating in the design and creation of the OnBase product.  He was named vice president of Latin American Sales in 2000, where he established and grew Hyland’s footprint in this region.  In 2007, Alfonso became vice president of international, during which Hyland extended its global footprint, and secured the first customers and partners in Romania, China and the Middle East. In 2009, Alfonso became vice president of Hyland’s Enterprise Solution Group, establishing a successful consulting practice that works in partnership with executives to assess their corporate initiatives specific to automating processes, reducing costs and achieving efficiency goals.

Before joining Hyland, Alfonso held multiple technical leadership positions including director of technical programs for One Net International, and technical program manager for the Cleveland Advanced Manufacturing Program (CAMP). Prior to that, he was a consultant for CAP Gemini Sogeti and vice president of engineering for Third Wave Technology.

Alfonso, a native of Peru, received a B.S. in Computer Engineering from Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland.
