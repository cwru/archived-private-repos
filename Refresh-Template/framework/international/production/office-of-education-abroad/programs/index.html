Find a Program

All programs can be found on the Program Search engine.

To search:

  • Select a term for your study abroad. Find examples of program terms.
  • Select your field of interest from the drop-down box for “Course Offerings."
  • Select a region or a country for your study abroad experience.
  • Once you select a program, use the blue “Click to Visit” button to find out about courses offered, housing options, internships or research opportunities, and more.

When you find your program, select “Apply Now” on the program page.

When you start an application, the Office of Education Abroad will be notified. If you have not yet had an advising appointment, you will then be emailed to schedule a meeting with a study abroad advisor. See application deadlines.

If you would like to meet with an advisor before beginning your application, email studyabroad@case.edu or stop into the Center for International Affairs in Tomlinson Hall, Room 143.
