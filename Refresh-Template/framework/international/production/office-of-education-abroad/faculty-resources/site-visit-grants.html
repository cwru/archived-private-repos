Site Visit Program

The Office of Education Abroad conducts site visits of all of our study abroad partners on a five-year cycle. Faculty and staff are invited to apply for site visit grants. These grants of up to $500 are meant to supplement a previously planned trip to the partner institution or the partner institution’s city.

For more information, visit the section within the Office of Global Strategy.
