Proposing New Semester Programs

Case Western Reserve University has a list of more than 80 approved semester programs from which students can choose to study abroad. These programs have been approved by the Education Abroad Advisory Council, a group of faculty from all four of the undergraduate schools who examine study abroad policies, procedures and programs so CWRU can offer high quality opportunities for our students.

We encourage faculty to recommend new semester programs, either as exchange programs or other types of partnerships. CWRU has a process for signing new international agreements with partner institutions. In addition to the international agreement process, all exchange partnerships are reviewed by the Education Abroad Advisory Council to ensure that we can maintain a student exchange balance and that the programs meet study abroad risk management requirements.

In order to recommend a new exchange agreement, please follow the international agreement process. If you have questions, do not hesitate to contact us at agreements@case.edu.
