Online System

The student application and management system, TerraDotta, is a comprehensive study abroad database that tracks students from the initial application until they return from their study abroad experience. Faculty taking students on programs can have access to their programs through the system to help manage study abroad risk-management information.

The Office of Education Abroad will work with you to set up your program. We encourage anyone using the system to attend a TerraDotta training session to get a complete overview of the system’s capabilities, the reports, and the best way to manage systems. Download specific instructions on how to log in, set up a course and run reports.
