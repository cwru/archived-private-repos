International Alumni

Stay in Touch with CWRU Alumni Around the World

Join Case Western Reserve University and let us help you stay in touch with your Spartan family. CWRU alumni come from more than 130 (as of January 2014) countries throughout the world. Stay connected with other alumni and the campus, learn about upcoming events, find career opportunities, assist with recruitment opportunities, and more. The university’s Alumni Association also has a list of international chapters for you to join after graduation.

International Alumni Liaisons

Founded to bridge alumni connections around the world, International Alumni Liaisons hold a vital role in Case Western Reserve University’s international outreach efforts and help enhance its global presence and reputation. International Alumni Liaisons act as a point of contact for information about their geographical region/country, for the benefit of CWRU alumni, faculty, staff or students.

Current students—either international or domestic students who are hoping to move abroad—often have questions and need geographic or culturally specific advice. Are you willing to be a resource to the campus community?

Beyond being a contact to current students, International Alumni Liaisons have the opportunity to become more involved at their discretion. There are opportunities to assist CWRU in recruiting: by visiting college fairs, meeting applicants, or even visiting your own high-school to discuss life at Case Western Reserve University. Faculty and staff forging connections abroad may also be interested in contacting you.

If you are a CWRU alumnus and would like to join as an International Alumni Liaison, please complete the interest form.
