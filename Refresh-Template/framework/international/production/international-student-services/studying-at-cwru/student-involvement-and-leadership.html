Student Involvement and Leadership

Get Involved at CWRU

There’s so much more to a college experience than just attending classes and getting good grades.

At Case Western Reserve University, there are a wealth of opportunities that promote student engagement. Whatever your passion, there’s either an existing student group or the opportunity for you to create one! Being involved on campus can help you foster friendships and enhance your learning experience here at CWRU.
