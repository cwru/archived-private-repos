Studying at CWRU

Case Western Reserve University is one of the nation’s leading research institutions, with faculty expertise ranging from neurology to nursing, political science to polymers, community development to constitutional law, dental medicine to design and innovation. Here, you can study nearly anything, from the top experts in their field.

Want to know more about what it’s like to be a student at Case Western Reserve University and what academic resources are available to international students?
