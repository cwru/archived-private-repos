Kaitlyn Lionti

Contact:

kaitlyn.lionti@case.edu

Phone: 216.368.2517

Coordinator of Special Projects

Kaitlyn Lionti is responsible for implementing strategic initiatives to promote the internationalization of Case Western Reserve University. As part of this effort, she provides support for the International Affairs Visiting Committee and helps facilitate international agreements.

Kaitlyn graduated from Kent State University with a degree in Broadcast Journalism. While at Kent State, she traveled to London and Paris to study media outside the United States.

After graduation, Kaitlyn spent nearly nine years working as a television news reporter in Upstate New York, where she developed an interest in higher education and the impact it has on the greater community.
