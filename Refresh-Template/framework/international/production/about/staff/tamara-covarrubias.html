Tamara Covarrubias

Contact:

tamara.covarrubias@case.edu

Phone: 216.368.2517

Coordinator of Special Projects

Tamara Covarrubias is responsible for helping to maintain the data and the systems that control them for our study abroad and international students. Additionally, she works with our data to identify trends and work on solutions to best aid our student populations.

Tamara graduated with her undergraduate degree in Business Management from the University of Málaga and earned an MBA from Baldwin Wallace University. She worked and studied abroad in both the US and Greece. Tamara is originally from Madrid, Spain and her greatest passion is international education.
