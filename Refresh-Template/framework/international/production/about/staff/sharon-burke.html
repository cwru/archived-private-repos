Executive Aide

Sharon Burke provides professional and administrative support for the full range of projects and activities carried out through the Office of International Affairs. She coordinates with constituencies inside and outside Case Western Reserve University, provides assistance to visiting international delegations, manages the office, plans and coordinates special events, and organizes the vice provost's schedule. She is a resource for all questions about the Center for International Affairs.

Before coming to CWRU in 2009, Sharon worked in advertising, promotions and public relations. She received her BA in Theatre Arts and Dance at Cleveland State University.
